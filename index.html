An app for managing your medicinal waste

Healr is a company that collects expired medicinal waste from different chemist shops, sorts them and sells them further to recyclers. Consequently, exchange of these medicinal waste assists in waste management and Healr. is able to give back a better and efficient output to the society.

Healr has the potential to reduce medicinal waste and lower the wealth that goes to trash.

Features

Pickup

After scheduling the pickup, users will have to drop off the medicinal waste at a nearby pharmacy on any day according to their convenience.

Collab

It allows all the Pharmacies in India to apply for a partnership in which they will be agreeing to collect expired meds from customers and in return get commission as well as promotion.

Remind

It is impossible for a human to remember all the expiry dates of all the Medicines kept at house. Well don't worry, the Remind feature will Remind you right on time.

Profile

It allows you to manage your Healr Credits as well as keep a track of your order history.
