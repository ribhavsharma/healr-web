An app to combat medical unawareness.

faid is the only product in the market that uses a convolutional neural network to best describe a person’s physical disease by scanning each and every pixel of the picture he/she clicks. Many people mistake one condition for another and end up taking the wrong steps to aid it. Hence, they further worsen their condition. These people are the target audience of our app and we are here to provide assistance to them.

We provide a 4-in-1 step-by-step process to help combat this increasing problem.


Features

Detect

The Detect feature allows the user to scan an area of his skin that he believes is looking different, and faid will show you the most probable disease that you might be having.

Diagnosis

The feature displays a list of symptoms, and you can choose the ones you’re feeling at the moment. The app then connects all the symptoms and displays a list of the most probable conditions/diseases you might be suffering from.

Learn

Once you get to know what disease you’re most probably suffering from using the Diagnosis feature, you can click on any one of the symptoms and it redirects you to the Google Card of the disease. The card features the treatments, causes, symptoms and various other information about the disease.

Nearby Clinics

This feature allows the user to see clinics nearest to his current location, if things get serious.
